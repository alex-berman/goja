In this first part of the experiment, you will listen to and rate excerpts of music (and possibly other kinds of sound). This part is expected to take approximately 5 minutes.

{% elif state == 'pre_pair' %}

Your ratings have been registered and will be analyzed by the AI.

You will now be paired up with another participant.

{% elif state == 'pair' %}

Waiting for another participant to become available...

{% elif state == 'pre_chat' %}

You have been paired up with another participant.

{% if role == 'operator' %}

In this second part of the experiment, you will be assigned the role of a test operator. This means that you will chat with the other participant about his/her test results. The test results and various information will be presented to you in a tool. The other participant does not have access to this tool.

{% elif role == 'respondent' %}

In this second part of the experiment, you will receive your test results. This will be done in the form of a chat with the other participant, who is given access to your test results and various information.

{% endif %}

Note that participation is anonymous. Please do not share any personal information (such as your name) in the chat.

The chat continues for as long as you and the other participant want. When you want to leave the conversation, and thereby the experiment, you can close the browser.

{% endif %}
